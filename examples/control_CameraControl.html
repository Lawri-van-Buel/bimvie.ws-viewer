Example: Controlling a BIMSURFER.Camera with a BIMSURFER.CameraControl

  • Drag mouse to rotate
  • Drag mouse with left and right buttons down to pan
  • Mouse wheel to zoom
  • Arrow keys to rotate
  • WSAD keys to pan
  • + and - keys to zoom
  • 1,2,3,4,5,6 keys to select axis-aligned preset views
